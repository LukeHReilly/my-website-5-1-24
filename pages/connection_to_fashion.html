Danny Brown's Connection to Fashion

Taking a look at Danny Brown's personal love for fashion, and his connection to it

Starting off, we have a list of Danny Brown's 25 favorite brands, from Complexs article on october 02,2013. This list includes brands that range from thrift-store finds to high-fashion. A few of the most interesting picks I found were Versace, which Brown says that his father was wearing Versace before Biggie was rapping about it in the 90's. ""My pops was wearing Versace and stuff before Biggie was rapping about it. Before it got popular in hip-hop culture. My pops had Versace. And you know what it is? It's just that Detroit, we're so big with the gators and shit. So you had these places that sold gators, these upscale places that sell these expensive-ass suits, and they would get expensive-ass shirts and shit too. That's when my dad got one. Not necessarily knowing that this was going to crack off in two years or a year later. And I didn't know how much the shirt cost." Next we have Dickies, which Danny Brown said was one of his favorites because his home city Detroit is a blue collar working city, and Dickies was very popular amongst blue collar workers in the area. And Pelle Pelle, which was one of the most interesting picks that I saw. Danny Brown said that he was more into the leathers in particular, and that Pelle Pelle was a big staple for when you were getting money in detroit.
